About Natural Farming Practices

Beejamrutham

A natural seed treatment made from cow dung, cow urine, water, lime, and soil. It protects seeds from fungal diseases and aids in germination.

PMDS (Pest Management Dashparni)

A fermented mixture of 10 different plant leaves known for their pest-repellent properties. It's a natural pesticide that controls a wide range of pests.

Inter/Mixed Cropping

Planting different crops in the same field. This improves soil health, increases biodiversity, and provides natural pest control.
